Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hazards ("App") collects, uses, and protects your information. The App is operated by CloudTrade OÜ ("we", "our", "us").

If you have any questions, you may contact us at: help@hazards.cloud

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

The App collects precise location data (GPS coordinates) when the user grants permission. Location data is collected while the app is in use (foreground).

Location information is used to:

  • Display hazard reports near the user's current position
  • Allow users to create location-based reports
  • Provide location-relevant safety alerts

Providing location data is optional. Users may disable location access at any time through their device settings. Disabling location may limit certain features of the App.

The App does not track users in the background when the app is closed.

Email Address

We collect your email address when you create an account in order to authenticate users and manage accounts.

Device and Technical Information

We may collect device identifiers, IP address, operating system information, and app usage data for security and performance purposes.

Push Notification Identifiers

We use OneSignal to send push notifications. This involves collecting a unique push notification identifier (OneSignal ID) linked to your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the service
  • Display hazard reports near your location
  • Allow users to create and manage reports
  • Send safety notifications and alerts
  • Improve app performance and security

3. Public Reports

Hazard reports submitted through the app may be publicly visible to other users. However, creating a report requires a registered account.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only with service providers necessary to operate the app, including:

  • OneSignal – for push notification delivery
  • Backend infrastructure providers – for hosting and database services

These providers process data only to provide services on our behalf.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly within the app or by contacting us at help@hazards.cloud. Upon deletion, your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems, except where retention is required by law.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at help@hazards.cloud.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
